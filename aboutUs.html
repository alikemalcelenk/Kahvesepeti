Brief History

Kahvesepeti is a coffee brand headquartered in İstanbul, Turkey. Founded in 2020 by Ali Kemal Çelenk, currently the company runs more than 1000 coffee houses in eleven countries: the UK, Ireland, Sweden, Poland, Cyprus, Croatia, Turkey, the UAE, Oman, and the United States.

We take pride in the art and craft of coffee

Our coffees are craft-roasted in small batches in Kadıköy, Istanbul.

Our mission is to foster a community with great coffee - a community of farmers, roasters, baristas, and coffee drinkers.
